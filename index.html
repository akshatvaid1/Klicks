FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers about implementing automated operating systems in your business.

How do you decide what to automate?

We start with an operational audit. We map your current processes, isolate repetitive tasks and manual bottlenecks, and only build automations for workflows where the return on investment is immediate and obvious.

What is a custom AI Operating System?

Unlike standard software or simple chatbots, a custom AI Operating System connects your existing databases, CRMs, phone lines, messaging channels, and internal files into a single, automated operational workflow.

How long does implementation take?

A standard implementation takes between 3 to 6 weeks, depending on the complexity of your custom integrations, the database structures, and the communication endpoints (voice/SMS) required.

How do integrations work with our existing CRM?

We connect directly to your active software tools—such as Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Google Workspace, or custom internal databases. You do not need to migrate databases or change your existing systems.

Is our customer data secure?

Yes. We implement enterprise-grade security protocols, secure vector isolation, and data encryption. Your proprietary information and customer data are fully protected and comply with modern security standards.

Do you train our staff to use the system?

Yes. We provide complete documentation, loom videos, and live walkthroughs for your team. Because the systems are built to run in the background, your staff will see the results inside your current CRM without changing how they work.
